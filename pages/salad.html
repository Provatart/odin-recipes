Fresh Garden Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • Lettuce or mixed greens
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Combine in a large bowl.
  3. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss well.
  5. Serve fresh and enjoy!
